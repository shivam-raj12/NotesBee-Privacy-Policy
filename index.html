Privacy Policy for NotesBee

Last Updated: February 08, 2026

NotesBee ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information Collection and Use

We only collect information that is necessary to provide the features of the app.

Account Information: If you choose to create an account, we collect your Name, Email Address, and Password. This information is used solely for authentication and to provide access to your synced data.

Notes Data (Synced): For logged-in users, your notes are automatically synced to our secure database to prevent data loss and allow access across devices.

Device Information (Optional): When using the "Report an Issue" feature, you may choose to include device details (Manufacturer, Model, Android Version, and API Level) to help us diagnose and fix technical problems. This is completely optional.

Crash Data: We may collect anonymous crash-related information using Firebase Crashlytics to identify app errors and improve stability. This data does not personally identify you and does not include the content of your notes or chats.

Logged-out Use: If you use the app without creating an account, we do not collect any personal data or usage analytics.

2. WhatsApp Chat Export Feature & Disclaimer

NotesBee provides a tool that allows users to view and save their own exported WhatsApp chats locally.

Official Relationship: NotesBee is NOT an official app of WhatsApp or Meta. We are not affiliated with, associated with, authorized by, endorsed by, or in any way officially connected with WhatsApp Inc., Meta Platforms, Inc., or any of their subsidiaries.

Local Processing Only: When you share a WhatsApp export ZIP file with NotesBee, all processing and storage take place locally on your device.

No Cloud Sync: Exported WhatsApp chats are never uploaded to our servers and are not synced with our database. We do not have access to your WhatsApp conversations.

3. Security Features

We value your trust and use industry-standard security measures through Appwrite to protect your data. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

  • Note Locking: You can protect your notes with a local password lock on your device for additional privacy.
  • Encryption: All communication between the app and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate and improve the app.

Appwrite is used for:

  • User Authentication
  • Cloud Database Storage

Appwrite securely stores your account credentials and synced notes.

Firebase Crashlytics is used for:

  • Monitoring application crashes
  • Identifying technical issues
  • Improving app performance and stability

Crash reports may include anonymous device information such as device model, operating system version, and crash logs. This data is collected automatically and is used strictly for debugging purposes. It does not include personal notes or WhatsApp chat content.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Account Deletion: You can delete your account directly from the app settings.

Manual Deletion Request: If you cannot log in but wish to delete your data, contact us at srdevelopsteam@gmail.com.

Permanent Removal: Once your account is deleted, all associated personal information and synced notes are permanently removed from our systems and cannot be recovered.
