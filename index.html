Privacy Policy for NotesBee

Last Updated: January 05, 2026

NotesBee ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information Collection and Use

We only collect information that is necessary to provide the features of the app.

Account Information: If you choose to create an account, we collect your Name, Email Address, and Password. This is used solely for authentication and to provide access to your synced data.

Notes Data (Synced): For logged-in users, your notes are automatically synced to our secure database to ensure you do not lose your data.

Device Information (Optional): When using the "Report an Issue" feature, you may choose to include device details (Manufacturer, Model, Android Version, and API Level) to help us troubleshoot technical bugs. This is strictly optional.

Logged-out Use: If you use the app without an account, we do not collect any personal data or usage analytics.

2. WhatsApp Chat Export Feature & Disclaimer

NotesBee provides a tool for users to view and save their own exported WhatsApp chats locally.

Official Relationship: NotesBee is NOT an official app of WhatsApp or Meta. We are not affiliated with, associated with, authorized by, endorsed by, or in any way officially connected with WhatsApp Inc., Meta Platforms, Inc., or any of their subsidiaries or affiliates.

Local Processing Only: When you share a WhatsApp export ZIP file with NotesBee, all processing and storage of that chat happen locally on your device.

No Cloud Sync: Exported WhatsApp chats are NEVER uploaded to our servers or synced with our database. We cannot see or access your imported WhatsApp conversations.

3. Security Features

We value your trust in providing us your information. We use industry-standard security measures through Appwrite to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

  • Note Locking: We provide a feature that allows you to set a password to lock your notes locally on your device for extra privacy.
  • Encryption: All data transmitted between the app and our secure database is encrypted using industry-standard protocols (HTTPS/TLS).

4. Third-Party Services

We use Appwrite as our backend service provider for:

  • User Authentication
  • Cloud Database Storage

Appwrite is used to securely store your account credentials and your synced notes. You can view Appwrite’s privacy practices on their official website.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Account Deletion: You can delete your account directly within the app settings.

Manual Deletion Request: If you are unable to log in but wish to have your data deleted, please contact us at notesbee@support.com.

Permanent Removal: Upon account deletion, all personal information and synced notes associated with your account are permanently deleted from our databases and cannot be recovered.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at:
